MAVENs closes May 2026 with four Q1 papers published <!--more-->
- Physical Review B,
- Physical Review Materials,
- ACS Applied Energy Materials, and
- RSC Advances.

Three of these papers are the independent work of group members, reflecting the group's growing capacity for self-driven research. The ACS Applied Energy Materials paper was carried out in collaboration with Prof. Pralay Kanti Santra at the Centre for Nano and Soft Matter Sciences (CeNS), Bangalore.

Congratulations to all contributing students, and our thanks to Prof. Santra for a productive collaboration.
